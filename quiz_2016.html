About the site

My intedend goals of the quiz and site.

To many times I have come across someone who swears to vote soley by what they heard from a friend of a friend, or from diluted news sources. I wanted to create a site that would utilize a quiz that people could use. This quiz will show them where they stand on certain issues and have that measured against where they stand with a Presidential Candidate.

Understand the grading process.

The quiz is twenty eight questions long. Using a simple grading system we can draw a conclusion on a graded score. I set up the quiz to follow a specific candidate. This will allow the quiz to be graded in a manner that we could dived the score to see where the person stood on the issues. Below I show a simple table describing this process.

  • 20-28 = Hillary Clinton
  • 14-19 = In Between
  • 0-13 = Donald Trump
